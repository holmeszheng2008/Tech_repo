Locations

Main Campus

DeGroote’s undergraduate programs, specialized graduate programs and PhD program are based on McMaster University’s main campus in Hamilton, ON.

Address

1280 Main Street West
Hamilton, Ontario
L8S 4M4

Google Map

Directions for getting to McMaster can be found on the main website. Go to the entrance on Sterling Street. Enter and then turn right.

Pay parking for visitors is available in lots B, C, D, E, F, G, H and the stadium. A parking map can be found on the McMaster parking website.

You can take a tour (virtual or in-person) of the McMaster campus.
